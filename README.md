Fit Desi
Fit Desi is a culturally tailored fitness and nutrition app designed to promote a healthier lifestyle through balanced, oil-free, sugar-free, milk-free, and 100% vegan recipes. The app integrates traditional Pakistani cuisine with modern dietary practices, offering wholesome meals without compromising on taste or health. It also provides essential tools like BMI calculation, daily calorie tracking, personalized meal planning, and progress tracking to support you in achieving your health goals.

Key Features
1. BMI Calculator with Health Risk Assessment  
   Calculate your Body Mass Index (BMI) and receive personalized health risk assessments based on your BMI category.
2. Daily Calorie Intake Calculator  
   Estimate your daily caloric needs using key factors like age, weight, height, gender, and activity level to achieve your fitness goals efficiently.
3. Meal Planning with Pakistani Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner, and Snack Options  
   Get access to culturally inspired, vegan meal options for every meal of the day. The app provides:
   - Oil-free, sugar-free, and milk-free recipes.
   - Traditional dishes reimagined to be healthier, without compromising on flavors.
   - Preset meal plans for weight loss, muscle gain, and heart health.
4. Progress Tracking with Weight and Measurement Logs  
   Log and track your weight and body measurements over time. Easily monitor your progress to stay motivated and adjust your plans as needed.

Recipes and Nutrition
All recipes provided in Fit Desi are 100% plant-based and crafted to align with vegan dietary principles. This includes:
- No Oil: Recipes that focus on flavor without using added oils or fats.
- No Sugar: Dishes naturally sweetened without refined sugars.
- No Milk: Dairy-free options that are both delicious and nourishing.
The app offers a variety of breakfast, lunch, dinner, and snack options, each with complete preparation instructions and detailed nutritional information to help you make informed choices.

How to Use
1. Navigate through the App: Use the sidebar to switch between the different sections like the BMI Calculator, Calorie Intake Calculator, Meal Planning, Progress Tracking, and Recall.
2. Calculate and Track: Calculate your BMI and daily calorie needs, then log your weight and measurements to track your progress.
3. Explore Meal Options: Choose a meal type from the dropdown to explore recipes. The app displays recipes in a tabular format for easy reference.
4. Customize Your Experience: Choose preset meal plans or create custom plans based on your preferences and goals.

Tech Stack
- Streamlit: Interactive and visually appealing user interface.
- Python: Core logic for BMI calculation, calorie estimation, progress tracking, and meal plan management.
- Pandas: For managing and displaying recipe and progress data in tabular form.
- Custom CSS: For enhanced visual aesthetics and layout styling.

 Installation
1. Clone the repository:
   ```bash
   git clone https://github.com/your-repo/fit-desi.git
   ```
2. Navigate to the project directory:
   ```bash
   cd fit-desi
   ```
3. Install dependencies:
   ```bash
   pip install -r requirements.txt
   ```
4. Run the app:
   ```bash
   streamlit run app.py
   ```
